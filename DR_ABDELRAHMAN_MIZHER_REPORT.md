# Report: Dr. Abdelrahman Mizher (الدكتور عبدالرحمن مزهر)

## Overview
Dr. Abdelrahman Mizher is a highly respected **Consultant Psychiatrist and Addiction Specialist** based in Amman, Jordan. With over 25 years of clinical experience, he is recognized for his expertise in treating a wide range of mental health disorders for both adults and children.

**Role in MindCare:** Founder

---

## Professional Profile

### **Education & Certifications**
*   **Medical Degree:** Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery (MBBS) from **Azerbaijan Medical University**, Baku (1990).
*   **Board Certification:** **Jordanian Board Certificate in Psychiatry** (2007).
*   **Residency:** Completed psychiatric residency at the **National Center for Mental Health (NCMH)** and Al Quds Private Hospital in Jordan.

### **Career History**
*   **Practitioner since 1995.**
*   **Psychiatrist:** Worked at the National Center for Mental Health and Elite Medical Center.
*   **Consultant:** Served at Mutmaenna Private Psychiatric Clinic (Dr. Tarek Habib Clinic).
*   **International Experience:** Worked as a consultant psychiatrist in Saudi Arabia (NWAFAH).
*   **Medical Leadership:** Served as the Medical Director of the Geriatric Center “Wadi Shita” (2015–2018).
*   **Founder:** Established and leads the **"Beautiful Mind" (Creative Minds)** clinic in Amman.

---

## Areas of Expertise
Dr. Mizher provides comprehensive mental health services, specializing in:
*   **Mood Disorders:** Depression, Bipolar Disorder.
*   **Anxiety Disorders:** Generalized Anxiety, Phobias, Panic Attacks, Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD).
*   **Addiction:** Treatment and rehabilitation for various forms of substance and behavioral addiction.
*   **Developmental & Behavioral Disorders:** ADHD (Hyperactivity), Autism Spectrum Disorder, and behavioral issues in children.
*   **Therapeutic Approaches:** 
    *   Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
    *   Analytical Therapy / Psychoanalysis
    *   Applied Behavioral Analysis (ABA)
*   **Specialized Counseling:** Family counseling, divorce counseling, anger management, and geriatric psychotherapy.
*   **Neuropsychological Evaluation:** Mental health assessments, intelligence measures, and personality scales.
